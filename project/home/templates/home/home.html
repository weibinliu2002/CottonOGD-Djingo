CottonOGD

CottonOGD is a comprehensive cotton multi-omics database. First, we integrated all publicly available cotton multi-omics data with our own projects, conducted unified analysis and quality control. The resulting valuable data resources are provided to the cotton research community through a user-friendly cotton omics interface.

Gene Search

Data Source

Our database integrates data from multiple sources including:

  • Publicly available cotton genome databases
  • Published research papers on cotton genomics
  • Our own sequencing and analysis projects
  • Collaborative research data from partner institutions

All data has undergone rigorous quality control and standardized processing to ensure consistency and reliability.
